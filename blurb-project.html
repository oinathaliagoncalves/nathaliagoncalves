BLURB - Tracking Reading App

I designed Blurb as a UI/UX concept project focused on creating a playful and expressive reading tracker. The goal was to bring personality and emotion to the reading app experience, combining functionality with bold visual identity. The project included user research, wireframes, and high-fidelity design to explore usability, visual hierarchy, and engagement.

Tools Used: Figma

Features:community feed with likes and comments, reading goals tracking, star ratings, and a login-free onboarding experience using local storage.
